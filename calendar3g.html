EXHIBITION: HOT '14 Plymouth University Degree Show

Immerse yourself in a celebration of highly creative talent and enterprise; the culmination of study and practice at Plymouth University.

This fantastic exhibition of work from final year students covers a wide spectrum of the Arts: 3D Design, Architecture, Digital Art and Technology, Fine Art, Graphic Communication with Typography, Illustration, Media Arts, Photography and TV Arts.
