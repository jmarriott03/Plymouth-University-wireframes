Undergraduate
open day

Discover Plymouth University at our undergraduate open day on Wednesday 25 June 2014.

Student ambassadors and members of staff will be available on campus to welcome you and help you get the most out of your visit.

OPEN DAY INFORMATION

Once you have booked your place on our open day, visit our pre-open day information page, where you can find the information we think you will need to know before embarking on your journey of discovery.

Do you require additional support on the day?

If you require a disabled parking space on campus (blue badge holders only) or require a BSL signer on the day, please remember to add your requirements to the special requirements field on the registration form.

OPEN DAY PROGRAMME

(details the times of subject talks, tours and drop in sessions)

A copy of the programme will be sent to the email address provided on your registration form one week before the event, in order to help you plan your day.  A copy will also be available to collect at registration.

We will also provide you with a downloadable open day planner and we recommend spending 10 minutes choosing the talks, tours and drop-in sessions you'd like to attend.

You can also keep up-to-date with all our latest news and events on Facebook , or join our open day conversation on the Student Room.
